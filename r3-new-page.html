An Extra Set of Eyes Around the House

The R3 camera is the ideal indoor WiFi security camera for looking in on the kids, furry friends, the nanny and much more!

Stay Connected with Two-Way Audio

The R3 Indoor WiFi Camera keeps you connected with anyone in the area via its built-in mic and speaker. Speak and listen via the free mobile app to say hi to the kids and be with the family even when you’re away.

Pan Tilt Lens

Control the camera's direction right from the app. The remote pan/tilt function allows you to use the directional pad on the app interface to pan the camera up to 355° horizentally and tilt the camera up to 75°. Gain your perfect view, no matter where you place the camera.

Exceptional Clarity – All Day and Night

Experience full HD 1080p clarity in both livestream and playback footage. Plus, IR night vision up to 33 feet keeps you covered in the dark – perfect for the baby room or anywhere you need that extra set of eyes.

One Easy App

The free LaView mobile app brings your home footage right to your fingertips with convenient features and customizable settings for your perfect home security experience.

Multi-View

The multi-view feature allows you to view all your LaView USA cameras in a convenient grid, right on your mobile device.

Motion Screenshot Log

To help find specific moments and footage with ease, scroll through the motion screenshot log to see a snapshot of anyone picked up by the camera, along with a date and timestamp.

Push Notifications

Receive instant push notifications when your camera detects motion to stay up to date on any important activity around your home.

Instant Playback Footage

The app gives you instant access to stored HD footage caught by motion events in the camera’s field of view.

Two Convenient Storage Options

Safe storage of your home security camera footage is of the highest importance. Safely store and record with either MicroSD or our low-cost Cloud Service.

MicroSD

Onboard microSD slot supports up to 128GB for simple and accessible storage.

Cloud Storage

Our optional, low-cost cloud service expands your data capacity and stores your all-important footage offsite, with high encryption for your safety.
